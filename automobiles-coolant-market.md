<p><p>The automobile coolant market comprises several types based on their chemical compositions. Inorganic additives are traditional coolants containing silicates and phosphates, providing corrosion protection. Organic acid technology (OAT) coolants use organic acids to inhibit corrosion with longer service intervals. Hybrid organic acid technology (HOAT) combines both inorganic and organic inhibitors for balanced performance. Additionally, "Others" category includes specialized or alternative formulations like water-based coolants and proprietary blends, catering to unique engine needs or environmental considerations.</p></p>
