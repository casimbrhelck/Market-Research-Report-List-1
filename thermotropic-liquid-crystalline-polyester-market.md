<p><p>The thermotropic liquid crystalline polyester market comprises several processing methods, including injection molding, extrusion molding, and others. Injection molding involves injecting molten polyester into a mold to create complex shapes, ideal for high-precision parts. Extrusion molding, on the other hand, forces polyester through a die to produce continuous profiles like films or sheets. Other methods may include blow molding or compression molding, catering to specific applications. Each type offers unique advantages, such as efficiency, design flexibility, and material properties.</p></p>
