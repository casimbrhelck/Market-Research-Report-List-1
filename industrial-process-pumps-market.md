<p><strong>The Industrial Process Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Industrial Process Pumps</li><li>Positive Displacement Industrial Process Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial process pumps market is mainly divided into two types: centrifugal and positive displacement pumps. Centrifugal industrial process pumps utilize rotational energy to move fluids, making them suitable for high-flow applications and delivering continuous flow. In contrast, positive displacement industrial process pumps operate by trapping a fixed volume of fluid and forcing it through the discharge, providing precise flow control and handling viscous fluids effectively. Each type is tailored for specific applications across various industries, ensuring efficiency and reliability in fluid management.</p></p>
