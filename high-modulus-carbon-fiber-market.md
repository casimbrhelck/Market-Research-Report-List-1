<p><p>The high modulus carbon fiber market is primarily categorized into two types: High Modulus (HM) Grade and Ultra High Modulus (UHM) Grade. HM Grade carbon fibers exhibit enhanced stiffness and strength compared to standard fibers, making them suitable for applications in aerospace, automotive, and sporting goods. UHM Grade carbon fibers, having even greater stiffness and lower elongation, are ideal for specialized applications in advanced aerospace components, medical equipment, and high-performance sporting gear, where maximum structural integrity is essential.</p></p>
