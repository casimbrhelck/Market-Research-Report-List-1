<p><strong>The Automotive Engine Pulley Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crank Pulley</li><li>Accessory Pulley</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive engine pulley market can be categorized into two main types: crank pulleys and accessory pulleys. Crank pulleys, also known as harmonic balancers, are connected to the engine's crankshaft, helping to manage vibrations and drive the timing belt or chain. Accessory pulleys, on the other hand, power various engine accessories like the alternator, water pump, and air conditioning compressor. Both types are essential for engine functionality, ensuring efficient operation and optimal performance of the vehicle.</p></p>
