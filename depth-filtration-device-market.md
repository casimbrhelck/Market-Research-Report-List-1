<p><strong>The Depth Filtration Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bottom-In–Top-Out Mode (Inline Configuration)</li><li>Bottom-In–Bottom-Out Mode (T-Configuration)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Depth filtration devices are essential in various industries for separating particles from liquids. In the Bottom-In–Top-Out mode (Inline Configuration), the feed enters from the bottom and the filtrate exits at the top, facilitating continuous flow and efficient filtration. Conversely, the Bottom-In–Bottom-Out mode (T-Configuration) allows both input and output from the bottom, providing more versatile filtration options but requiring additional space. These configurations cater to different operational needs, enhancing performance and adaptability in filtration processes.</p></p>
