<p><strong>The Copper CMP Slurries Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prestonian Type</li><li>Non-Prestonian Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Copper CMP (Chemical Mechanical Polishing) slurries are essential for semiconductor manufacturing, with two primary market types: Prestonian and Non-Prestonian. Prestonian slurries adhere to the Preston equation, where polishing rate is directly proportional to pressure and slurry composition, resulting in consistent performance. In contrast, Non-Prestonian slurries exhibit less predictable behavior, where factors such as abrasive size or viscosity significantly influence the polishing process. These distinctions impact their applications, performance, and efficiency in integrated circuit fabrication.</p></p>
