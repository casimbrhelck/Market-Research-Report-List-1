<p><strong>The Colonic Cleansing Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open System Machine</li><li>Close System Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Colonic cleansing devices are categorized into two main types: open system machines and closed system machines. Open system machines feature a more natural approach, allowing waste to flow freely into a toilet, offering user comfort and a direct connection to the plumbing. Closed system machines, on the other hand, are designed for in-office use and involve a sealed system where waste is collected in a receptacle, providing more control and safety during the cleansing process. Both types aim to promote colon health and detoxification.</p></p>
