<p><strong>The Cuttings Re-injection Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dedicated Injection</li><li>Annular Injection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cuttings re-injection services involve the disposal of drill cuttings through specialized methods, primarily to minimize environmental impact. The market comprises two key types: Dedicated Injection, where cuttings are injected into designated wells specifically designed for this purpose, ensuring efficient containment and minimal leakage. Annular Injection, on the other hand, involves injecting cuttings into the annular space between the wellbore and casing, enabling effective waste management while maintaining well integrity. Both methods aim to promote sustainable drilling practices.</p></p>
