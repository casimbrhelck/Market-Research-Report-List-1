<p><ul><li>Organic Gum</li><li>Inorganic Glue</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The insulation sealant market is primarily divided into two types: organic gum and inorganic glue. Organic gum sealants are derived from natural sources, offering flexibility and excellent adhesion properties, making them suitable for various applications in construction and automotive industries. In contrast, inorganic glue sealants, often based on silicates or epoxy, provide superior thermal resistance and durability, making them ideal for high-temperature and industrial applications. Each type serves distinct needs, influencing their usage in insulation solutions.</p></p>
