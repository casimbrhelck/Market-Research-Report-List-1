<p><p>The static smart meter market consists of two primary types: Direct Current (DC) meters and Alternating Current (AC) meters. DC meters are designed for applications involving direct current flow, commonly used in renewable energy systems such as solar panels. In contrast, AC meters are more prevalent, measuring alternating current typically found in residential and commercial electrical grids. Both types leverage advanced technology for precise energy measurement, enhancing energy management and consumption tracking for users while supporting smart grid initiatives.</p></p>
