<p><p>The biological indicators for sterilization market primarily includes self-contained biological indicators and biological indicator strips. Self-contained biological indicators offer a convenient, single-unit solution that combines a microorganism with a growth medium, requiring no additional handling. In contrast, biological indicator strips consist of a strip of paper or plastic impregnated with spores, typically requiring external culture media for incubation. Both types are crucial for validating sterilization processes by confirming the effectiveness of sterilization methods in healthcare and industrial settings.</p></p>
