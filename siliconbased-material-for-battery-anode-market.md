<p><ul><li>"SiO/C"</li><li>"Si/C"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicon-based material market for battery anodes primarily includes SiO/C (silicon oxide/carbon) and Si/C (silicon/carbon) composites. SiO/C combines silicon oxide with carbon to enhance the electrochemical performance and improve cycle stability, making it suitable for high-capacity lithium-ion batteries. In contrast, Si/C consists of silicon particles embedded in a carbon matrix, allowing for efficient lithium-ion transport and better mechanical resilience. Both types aim to leverage silicon's high capacity while mitigating challenges like volume expansion during charging.</p></p>
