<p><ul><li>Optical Line Terminal</li><li>Optical Network Terminal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GPON equipment market consists primarily of two key components: Optical Line Terminals (OLT) and Optical Network Terminals (ONT). OLTs are located at the service provider's central office and manage data flow to multiple ONTs, serving as the gateway to the optical network. ONTs are installed at customer premises, converting optical signals into electrical signals for end-user devices. Together, they enable high-speed broadband access, supporting applications like internet, voice, and video services over a passive optical network infrastructure.</p></p>
