<p><ul><li>Strip-Cut</li><li>Cross-Cut</li><li>Micro-Cut</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paper shredder market comprises three primary types: strip-cut, cross-cut, and micro-cut. Strip-cut shredders slice paper into long strips, providing basic security suitable for general use. Cross-cut shredders offer enhanced protection by cutting paper into smaller particles, making reconstruction difficult and ideal for sensitive documents. Micro-cut shredders deliver the highest level of security, shredding paper into tiny pieces, ensuring maximum confidentiality, particularly for highly sensitive information. Each type serves distinct needs in document security and privacy.</p></p>
