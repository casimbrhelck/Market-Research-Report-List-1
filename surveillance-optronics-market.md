<p><p>The surveillance optronics market encompasses various technologies, primarily categorized into multispectral and hyperspectral imaging. Multispectral imaging captures data across several wavelengths, enhancing target detection by highlighting features invisible to the naked eye. In contrast, hyperspectral imaging collects and processes information across a broader spectrum, allowing for detailed material identification and analysis. Both technologies play crucial roles in applications such as defense, environmental monitoring, and agriculture, enabling enhanced situational awareness and improved decision-making based on rich spectral information.</p></p>
