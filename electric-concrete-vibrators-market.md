<p><p>The electric concrete vibrators market is segmented into three main types: internal vibrators, external vibrators, and others. Internal vibrators, designed to be inserted into the concrete mix, ensure thorough compaction and reduce air pockets. External vibrators, mounted on the formwork, enhance the flow of concrete without direct contact, making them ideal for larger structures. The "others" category includes specialized devices like surface vibrators and handheld models, catering to specific applications and user preferences in concrete consolidation.</p></p>
