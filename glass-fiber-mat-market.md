<p><strong>The Glass Fiber Mat Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet-Laid Process</li><li>Dry-Laid Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass fiber mat market encompasses production techniques like the wet-laid and dry-laid processes. The wet-laid process involves mixing glass fibers with water and an additive to form a mat, resulting in a uniform and high-density product, ideal for applications requiring high strength. In contrast, the dry-laid process relies on air to disperse glass fibers before bonding them with a binder, producing lighter mats suitable for more flexible applications. Both methods serve diverse industries such as construction, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
