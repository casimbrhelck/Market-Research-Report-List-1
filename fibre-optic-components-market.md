Active optical cables integrate transceivers to enhance performance and connectivity. Transceivers convert electrical signals to optical signals and vice versa. Amplifiers boost signal strength for longer ranges. Circulators manage signal direction, ensuring efficient transmission. Connectors provide critical junctions for joining fiber segments, enabling reliable communication in networks across diverse applications.</p></p>
