<p><p>Mobile banking market types are primarily categorized into cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based mobile banking leverages internet-hosted services, offering scalability, easy updates, and lower infrastructure costs, making it suitable for financial institutions seeking flexibility and faster deployment. In contrast, on-premise mobile banking involves hosting software locally, providing enhanced control over data security and customization, but requiring higher upfront costs and ongoing maintenance. Each type serves distinct needs, with cloud solutions appealing to digital-first banks and on-premise systems favored by traditional institutions.</p></p>
