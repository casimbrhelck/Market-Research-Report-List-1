<p><strong>The CNC Lathes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical</li><li>Horizontal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CNC lathes operate within two primary market types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical CNC lathes are designed with the spindle oriented vertically, allowing for better access and efficiency when machining larger and heavier workpieces. They excel in heavy-duty operations and are ideal for industries requiring precise vertical turning. Conversely, horizontal CNC lathes feature a spindle positioned horizontally, facilitating simpler setups and faster workpiece removal, making them suitable for high-volume production and smaller, intricate parts in diverse manufacturing settings.</p></p>
