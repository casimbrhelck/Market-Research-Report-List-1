<p><ul><li>Piston Type</li><li>Hydraulic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical pumps market comprises primarily two types: piston type and hydraulic type. Piston pumps utilize a reciprocating mechanism to generate pressure, making them ideal for high-pressure applications and fluid transfer. They are known for their efficiency and versatility in handling various fluids. Hydraulic pumps, on the other hand, use hydraulic energy to facilitate fluid movement, commonly employed in construction and industrial machinery. Both types cater to diverse industries, emphasizing reliability and performance in fluid management solutions.</p></p>
