<p><p>The biodegradable synthetic polymers market includes several key types, each with unique properties and applications. Polyglycolic Acid (PGA) is known for its strength and is often used in medical sutures. Polylactic Acid (PLA) is popular in packaging and 3D printing due to its biocompatibility and compostability. Polyhydroxyalkanoates (PHA) are versatile and produced by microbial fermentation, making them useful in various applications, including food packaging. Polybutylene Succinate (PBS) offers similar benefits and is used in agricultural and consumer products, promoting sustainability.</p></p>
