<p><p>The torpedo market is segmented into guided and unguided types. Guided torpedoes utilize advanced navigation systems, allowing them to adjust their trajectory in real-time to effectively target submarines and surface vessels, enhancing accuracy and effectiveness. Unguided torpedoes, on the other hand, rely on preset trajectories, making them simpler and often less expensive but less precise. Both types are utilized in various naval operations, with the choice depending on mission requirements, budget constraints, and the level of technological sophistication desired.</p></p>
