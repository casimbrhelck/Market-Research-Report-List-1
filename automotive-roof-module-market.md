<p><p>The Automotive Roof Module Market is categorized into two primary types: softtop and hardtop. Softtop roofs, typically made from flexible materials, are lightweight and often utilized in convertible vehicles, allowing for an open-air experience. Hardtop roofs, on the other hand, are constructed from rigid materials, providing enhanced durability, insulation, and security. Each type caters to different consumer preferences, with softtops appealing for style and versatility, while hardtops offer robustness and advanced features, influencing the overall automotive design and functionality.</p></p>
