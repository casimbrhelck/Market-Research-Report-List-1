<p><ul><li>One-Component Sealant</li><li>Two-Component Sealant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane insulating glass sealant market is segmented into one-component and two-component sealants. One-component sealants are ready-to-use products that cure through moisture absorption, making them convenient for quick application. They are commonly used for residential and light commercial purposes. In contrast, two-component sealants consist of a base and a hardener, requiring mixing before application. They offer enhanced performance and durability, making them ideal for high-demand environments, such as large commercial buildings and industrial applications.</p></p>
