<p><p>The Hepatitis C virus (HCV) antiviral market consists of three main types: HCV antibody tests, HCV viral load tests, and HCV genotyping tests. HCV antibody tests are used to detect antibodies produced in response to HCV infection, indicating past exposure. HCV viral load tests measure the amount of virus present in the bloodstream, which helps assess the infection's severity. HCV genotyping determines the specific strain of the virus, guiding treatment decisions and predicting response to antiviral therapy.</p></p>
