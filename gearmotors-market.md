<p><p>The gearmotors market comprises various types, including helical, helical-bevel, planetary, and worm gearmotors. Helical gearmotors offer high efficiency and smooth operation, while helical-bevel gearmotors provide a compact design with right-angle rotation. Planetary gearmotors are known for their high torque and power density, making them suitable for space-constrained applications. Worm gearmotors excel in achieving high reduction ratios and self-locking capabilities. Additionally, the "others" category includes specialized gearmotors tailored for specific industrial needs, ensuring a versatile solution for diverse applications.</p></p>
