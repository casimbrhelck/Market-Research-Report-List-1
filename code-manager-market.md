<p><p>Code managers operate in two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based code managers offer flexibility and scalability, allowing teams to access their code repositories and collaborate remotely via the internet. This model reduces infrastructure costs and maintenance efforts. In contrast, on-premises code managers provide organizations with more control over their data and security, as everything is hosted within their own infrastructure. This option is often preferred by enterprises with strict compliance requirements or those managing sensitive information.</p></p>
