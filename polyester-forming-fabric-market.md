<p><p>The Polyester Forming Fabric market encompasses various types designed for different paper production processes. The 16-shed double-and-a-half layer fabric maximizes strength and performance for demanding applications. Lastly, SSB (Structured Surface Binding) forming fabrics optimize sheet formation, ensuring superior surface quality and efficiency in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
