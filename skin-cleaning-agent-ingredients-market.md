<p><p>The skin cleaning agent market is categorized into three main types: anionic, non-ionic, and amphoteric cleaners. Anionic cleaners are highly effective at removing dirt and oils, making them popular in personal care products. Non-ionic cleaners are milder, offering gentle cleansing without harsh effects, suitable for sensitive skin. Amphoteric cleaners, which can act as either anionic or cationic depending on pH, provide versatile cleansing options and are common in various formulations, balancing effectiveness with skin compatibility.</p></p>
