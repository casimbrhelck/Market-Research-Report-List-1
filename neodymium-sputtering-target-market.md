<p><p>The Neodymium sputtering target market is primarily segmented into two types: rotary and non-rotatable targets. Rotary targets are cylindrical and can continuously rotate during the sputtering process, enhancing uniformity and efficiency in material deposition. They are typically used in high-volume applications. On the other hand, non-rotatable targets are stationary and are often employed in smaller-scale production or specialized applications. Both types serve crucial roles in various industries, including electronics and renewable energy, depending on the specific requirements of the deposition process.</p></p>
