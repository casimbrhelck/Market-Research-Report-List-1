<p><strong>The 2 Piece Aluminum Can Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DRD (Draw and Redraw) Cans</li><li>DWI (Drawn and Wall Ironed) Cans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The two-piece aluminum can market is primarily divided into Draw and Redraw (DRD) cans and Drawn and Wall Ironed (DWI) cans. DRD cans are produced by drawing a flat aluminum sheet into a cup shape and then redrawing it to achieve the desired dimensions, offering a lightweight and cost-effective solution. DWI cans undergo a more complex process where the drawn cup is ironed to create a thinner, uniform wall, enhancing strength and allowing for innovative designs and improved aesthetics, making them suitable for premium beverages.</p></p>
