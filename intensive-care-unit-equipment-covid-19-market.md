<p><p>The Intensive Care Unit (ICU) equipment market for COVID-19 includes various types of devices essential for patient management. Invasive ventilators support patients with severe respiratory failure by delivering controlled breaths. Non-invasive ventilators assist those with milder respiratory distress without intubation. ECMO equipment provides extracorporeal membrane oxygenation for critically ill patients, facilitating blood oxygenation. Independent aspirators are used to clear secretions from the airway, enhancing breathing function. Additionally, the "Others" category encompasses various supportive devices vital for COVID-19 care.</p></p>
