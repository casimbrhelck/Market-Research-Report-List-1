<p><strong>The OLED Display Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emitting Layer (EML)</li><li>Hole Transport Layer (HTL)</li><li>Electron Transport Layer (ETL)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OLED display materials market consists of several key components tailored for optimal performance. The Emitting Layer (EML) is crucial for light emission, containing organic compounds that produce light when excited. The Hole Transport Layer (HTL) facilitates the movement of holes (positive charges) towards the EML, enhancing efficiency. The Electron Transport Layer (ETL) allows electrons to flow towards the EML, aiding in recombination for light production. Additional materials, such as substrates and encapsulants, support overall display functionality and durability.</p></p>
