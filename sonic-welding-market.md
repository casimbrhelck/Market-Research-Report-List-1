<p><strong>The Sonic Welding Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Strike Ultrasonic Bonding</li><li>Continuous Sonic Welding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sonic welding market comprises two main types: Single-Strike Ultrasonic Bonding and Continuous Sonic Welding. Single-Strike Ultrasonic Bonding involves applying a brief burst of ultrasonic energy to create strong, localized welds, making it ideal for low-volume, high-precision applications. In contrast, Continuous Sonic Welding utilizes a steady flow of ultrasonic energy to bond materials over a longer duration, facilitating high-speed production for larger volumes. Both methods offer consistent, reliable joints suitable for various industries, including automotive, medical, and consumer goods.</p></p>
