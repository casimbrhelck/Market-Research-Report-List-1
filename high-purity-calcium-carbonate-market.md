<p><p>The High Purity Calcium Carbonate market is primarily segmented into two types: uncoated precipitated calcium carbonate (PCC) and coated precipitated calcium carbonate. Uncoated PCC is valued for its high brightness and purity, making it suitable for various applications including pharmaceuticals and food products. In contrast, coated PCC has a surface treatment that enhances its dispersibility and compatibility with polymers, making it ideal for use in coatings, plastics, and paper industries. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs and applications.</p></p>
