<p><p>The Voltage Controlled Oscillator (VCO) market includes various types of oscillators, primarily quartz oscillators and silicon oscillators. Quartz oscillators utilize crystalline quartz to maintain precise frequency stability, making them ideal for applications requiring accuracy, such as telecommunications and clocks. Silicon oscillators, on the other hand, leverage semiconductor technology to achieve compact size and integration with digital circuits, often used in consumer electronics and IoT devices. Both types play crucial roles in signal generation and frequency modulation across numerous electronic applications.</p></p>
