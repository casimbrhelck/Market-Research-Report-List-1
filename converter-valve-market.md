<p><p>The Converter Valve Market is segmented into three main types based on voltage levels: Ultra High Voltage (UHV), Extra High Voltage (EHV), and High Voltage (HV). UHV solutions operate at voltage levels above 800 kV, facilitating long-distance power transmission with minimal losses. EHV designs range from 345 kV to 800 kV, suitable for regional networks. HV systems operate between 100 kV to 345 kV, commonly used in local networks. Each type addresses specific transmission needs and efficiency requirements in the power grid.</p></p>
