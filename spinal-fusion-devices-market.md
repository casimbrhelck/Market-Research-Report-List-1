<p><ul><li>Thoraco-lumbar Fixation Devices</li><li>Cervical Fixation Devices</li><li>Interbody Fusion Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spinal fusion devices market comprises three main types: thoraco-lumbar fixation devices, cervical fixation devices, and interbody fusion devices. Thoraco-lumbar fixation devices are used to stabilize the thoracic and lumbar spine, preventing movement and promoting fusion. Cervical fixation devices focus on the cervical spine, ensuring stability in the neck region. Interbody fusion devices are implanted between vertebrae to support spinal alignment and facilitate fusion. Together, these devices address various spinal conditions, enhancing patient outcomes through improved stabilization and healing.</p></p>
