<p><p>The automotive pneumatic equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: Compressed Air Type and Compressed Inert Gases Type. </p><p>Compressed Air Type utilizes atmospheric air, compressed to power tools and equipment, improving efficiency in various automotive processes. This type is widely used for tasks like tire inflation, painting, and powering pneumatic tools. </p><p>Compressed Inert Gases Type involves gases such as nitrogen or argon, which are used for specialized applications, including tire inflation and protecting sensitive automotive components, ensuring safety and maintaining optimal performance.</p></p>
