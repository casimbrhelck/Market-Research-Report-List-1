<p><ul><li>Bunk Trailers</li><li>Float-on Trailers</li><li>Roller Trailers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Boat handling trailers are essential for transporting watercraft and come in three main types: bunk trailers, float-on trailers, and roller trailers. Bunk trailers feature padded bunks that support the boat's hull, providing stability during transport. Float-on trailers allow boats to be floated onto the trailer over a shallow ramp, making loading easier. Roller trailers utilize a series of rollers to aid in launching and retrieving boats, reducing friction and facilitating movement. Each type serves specific needs based on boat design and usage.</p></p>
