<p><p>Quality Management Platforms (QMPs) are categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based QMPs offer flexibility and scalability, allowing users to access the system from anywhere with an internet connection, which promotes collaboration and reduces IT overhead. On-premises QMPs, on the other hand, are installed locally on a company’s hardware, providing greater control over data security and compliance but typically requiring higher upfront costs and maintenance. Both types cater to varying organizational needs and preferences.</p></p>
