<p><ul><li> Fully Covered Esophageal Stent</li><li>Partially Covered Esophageal Stent </li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-expandable covered esophageal stent market consists of two main types: fully covered esophageal stents and partially covered esophageal stents. Fully covered stents provide complete coverage with a material that prevents tissue ingrowth, offering better patient outcomes and reducing complications. In contrast, partially covered stents have exposed ends, allowing some tissue integration while still providing a barrier to strictures. Each type is designed to address different clinical needs in treating esophageal disorders, such as strictures or malignant obstructions.</p></p>
