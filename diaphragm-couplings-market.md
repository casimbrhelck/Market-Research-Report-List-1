<p><strong>The Diaphragm Couplings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Diaphragm</li><li>Double Diaphragm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diaphragm couplings are flexible coupling devices that connect two shafts while allowing for misalignment and reducing vibrations. The single diaphragm type utilizes a single plate to transmit torque, making it suitable for compact applications with moderate misalignment. In contrast, double diaphragm couplings feature two plates, enabling higher torque capacity and improved flexibility, ideal for heavier loads and greater misalignment scenarios. Both types are widely used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing for reliable and efficient power transfer.</p></p>
