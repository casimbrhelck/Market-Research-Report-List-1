<p><strong>The Cold Plasma Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Atmospheric Cold Plasma</li><li>Low-Pressure Cold Plasma</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cold plasma market is typically divided into two main types: atmospheric cold plasma and low-pressure cold plasma. Atmospheric cold plasma operates at normal atmospheric pressure and is widely used for surface treatment, sterilization, and food processing due to its ability to interact directly with materials. In contrast, low-pressure cold plasma functions in a vacuum setting, making it suitable for semiconductor manufacturing, thin film deposition, and medical applications, where precise control over the plasma environment is crucial.</p></p>
