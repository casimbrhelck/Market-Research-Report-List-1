<p><p>The Expanded Polytetrafluoroethylene (ePTFE) microporous membranes market is segmented into micron and submicron types based on pore size. Micron membranes have larger pore sizes, typically suitable for applications like air and liquid filtration, allowing for efficient separation of particles while maintaining high permeability. In contrast, submicron membranes feature finer pores, offering enhanced filtration capabilities for smaller particles and pathogens, making them ideal for applications in medical devices, pharmaceuticals, and environmental protection, where high purity is essential.</p></p>
