<p><p>The automotive starting battery market primarily consists of two main types: maintenance-free batteries and conventional batteries. Maintenance-free batteries, often absorbed glass mat (AGM) or gel types, require little to no upkeep and offer enhanced durability and performance, making them ideal for modern vehicles with high electrical demands. In contrast, conventional batteries, typically flooded lead-acid types, require regular maintenance, including water level checks, but are more affordable. Both types cater to different consumer needs and preferences in the automotive sector.</p></p>
